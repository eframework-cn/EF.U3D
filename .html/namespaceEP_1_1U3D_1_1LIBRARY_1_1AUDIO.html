◆ AudioPickSubItemMode

enum EP.U3D.LIBRARY.AUDIO.AudioPickSubItemMode

Used by AudioItem to determine which AudioSubItem is chosen.

Enumerator
Disabled 

disables playback

Random 

chooses a random subitem with a probability in proportion to AudioSubItem.Probability

RandomNotSameTwice 

chooses a random subitem with a probability in proportion to AudioSubItem.Probability and makes sure it is not played twice in a row (if possible)

Sequence 

chooses the subitems in a sequence one after the other starting with the first

SequenceWithRandomStart 

chooses the subitems in a sequence one after the other starting with a random subitem

AllSimultaneously 

chooses all subitems at the same time

TwoSimultaneously 

chooses two different subitems at the same time (if possible)

StartLoopSequenceWithFirst 

always chooses the first subitem

RandomNotSameTwiceOddsEvens 

Same as RandomNotSameTwice but only picks from odds or evens switching every time. Useful for footsteps left/right.
